About Me

Hey! I'm Brandon, a mostly self-taught Front End Web Developer based in Oswego, Illinois. I am currently seeking for a full time position and ready and willing to relocate anywhere I am needed.

I graduated from Bradley University in August 2015 with a Bachelor's of Science. I majored in Interactive Media with a focus in Game Design (Interactive Media was a combination of Game Design, Web Design, and Animation all in one program, allowing you to choose which one you focus in).

I tried to focus on Game Design, specifically the art side of it(2D Art/Animations, 3D Modeling, etc.), but although I love playing video games, I did not love making them.

After coming to this realization, I remembered how much I enjoyed Web Development at school. Even though I only learned the very basics since I didn't focus in Web Design, I was still very much interested in it, so I decided to teach myself more and start my career search in the right field for me.

I'm excited to learn even more and begin this new chapter in life and I hope to hear from you soon!
